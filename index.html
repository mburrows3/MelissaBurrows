A little bit about me.

I'm finally pursuing my passion. I am based out of the Chicago suburbs and I have a deep longing for a more enriching career. I have spent the last 6 years raising up classrooms of unique, talented students from K-2nd grade who have been identified as having an Emotional Disability. I poured my energy into providing the best education as well as the safest environment for them.

I found that my energy ran out quick in that position, and I wasn't using the parts of my brain that make me feel alive. It was time I embarked on a new journey. A door opened to learn Web Development and I ran through. I have a need to learn and continue to stretch my mind in new, innovative ways. I have not found anything that fulfills that need as well as this.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
